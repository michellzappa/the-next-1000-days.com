Not a week goes by without a major corporation announcing groundbreaking developments in their AI approach. For better or worse, we are experiencing a transition of business models and value generation which relies on increased automation against a decreasing surface area of attention.

Our ability to absorb information shows no signs of increasing together with the glut of data these systems generate, leaving more people short on energy to even try keeping up. I’m no exception, and believe the only sensible way forward is building systems that help us nurture our underlying humanity, imagination and judgement. This week’s links hope to capture that sentiment through the words (and memes) of others.
