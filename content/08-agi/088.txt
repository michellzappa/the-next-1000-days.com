# Sparks of AGI


There have allegedly been sparks of AGI even in GPT-4. This was featured literally in the first edition of the newsletter, which talks about how GPT-4 is showing initial capabilities of having a generalized capability set. For example, understanding complex instructions and being able to direct the output in novel or unexpected directions.
