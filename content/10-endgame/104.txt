# Context Windows

{Context Window}

Context windows in AI define the relevant information span a model considers when generating responses. By optimizing context windows, businesses can ensure their AI systems harness essential data efficiently, enhancing accuracy and relevance in outputs. This capability improves customer interactions, boosts productivity, and drives informed decision-making across various business applications.

---

# COT

{CoT (Chain of Thought)}

Chain of Thought (CoT) reasoning in AI enhances decision-making by mimicking human cognitive processes. This approach encourages models to articulate their reasoning step-by-step, improving transparency and interpretability. For businesses, CoT aids in complex problem-solving, fostering trust in AI-generated insights and facilitating better strategic planning and risk management.
